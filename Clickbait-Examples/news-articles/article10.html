City Expands Bike Lane Network for Cyclists

The city has added 12 miles of new bike lanes to its downtown area, part of an ongoing commitment to sustainable, safe transportation. Cyclist advocacy groups have voiced strong support for the initiative, citing reduced accidents and pollution.
