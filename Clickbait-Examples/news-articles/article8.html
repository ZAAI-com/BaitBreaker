Because it’s rich in fiber, balances blood sugar, and helps curb cravings before they strike. Yet, few people credit it for their transformations. The medical establishment is finally taking notice.

Why the Experts Are Shocked

Dietician Dr. Haruto Watanabe adds, “It’s not magic, it’s chemistry. The trick is making healthy default choices easy and delicious. The apple was hiding in plain sight.”
